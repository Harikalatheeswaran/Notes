# Body Recomposition System: 7-9 Month Elite Protocol

## 1. Weekly Schedule & Training Split
This 4-day **Upper/Lower Split** maximizes muscle protein synthesis by hitting each muscle group twice per week while allowing for recovery during office/WFH transitions.

* **Monday (Office):** Upper A (Push/Pull focus)
* **Tuesday (Office):** Lower A (Posterior Chain & Quads)
* **Wednesday:** Rest / Active Recovery
* **Thursday (WFH):** Upper B (Hypertrophy/Volume focus)
* **Friday (WFH):** Lower B (Strength & Core)
* **Weekend:** Rest / Zone 2 Cardio (Long walks)

## 3. Exercise Guidance

### Incline Dumbbell Press (Target: Upper Chest)

```
[ / ]   <-- DBs at 45-degree angle
   \O/    <-- Shoulder blades retracted
    |     <-- Bench at 30 degrees
   / \
```
* **Cue:** Imagine "shoving" your back into the bench while pushing the weights up. Keep elbows tucked at 45 degrees.

### Lat Pulldown (Target: Back Width)

```
__________ <-- Wide Bar
     /| \
    ( O )    <-- Pull to collarbone
     / \
```

* **Cue:** Drive your elbows down toward your back pockets. Do not use your hands as "hooks"; pull with the elbows.

## 4. Cardio Strategy (Post-Weightlifting Only)
* **Protocol:** 20 minutes of LISS (Low-Intensity Steady State).
* **Method:** Treadmill walking at a 3.0–5.0 incline.
* **Speed:** 5.0–5.5 km/h.
* **Why:** This burns fat while preserving glycogen and energy for heavy lifting. Never do high-intensity cardio *before* lifting.

---

## 5. Strength Progression: Pull-up & Push-up Protocol

### The First Pull-up
1.  **Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4):** Scapular Pull-ups (3 sets x 12 reps). Learn to engage the lats without bending arms.
2.  **Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8):** Negative Pull-ups. Use a stool to jump to the top, then lower yourself for a 5-second count.
3.  **Phase 3:** Band-assisted pull-ups once negatives are controlled.

### Push-up Expansion
* End every Upper session with 2 sets of Push-ups to failure.
* Once you hit 20 reps per set, place a 2.5 kg or 5 kg plate on your upper back to continue the overload.

## 6. Fat Loss & Nutrition Strategy
* **Belly Fat Myth:** You cannot "spot reduce." Fat loss is systemic. To lose love handles, your total body fat must drop via a slight caloric deficit.
* **Protein Requirement:** Target **140g–160g daily**. You must swap current food for lean poultry, fish, Greek yogurt, or whey protein. Two eggs (12g protein) is insufficient for muscle gain.
* **Calorie Logic:** Since your weight is stable at 86 kg, simply increasing protein and the intensity of this 4-day plan will trigger recomposition without needing a drastic "starvation" diet.

---

## 7. Progressive Overload System
* **The Rule:** If you hit the top of the rep range (e.g., 10 reps) for all 3 sets with perfect form, you **must** increase the weight in the next session.
* **Increments:** +2.5 kg for Upper Body; +5 kg for Lower Body.
* **Tracking:** Log every session. If the numbers don't go up over time, you aren't building muscle.

---

## 8. Recovery & Optimization
* **Sleep:** Increase to 7.5+ hours. Muscle is built during sleep via Growth Hormone release.
* **Deload:** Every 8th week, reduce the weight of all exercises by 30% to allow your joints and nervous system to recover.
* **Beginner Mistake:** Changing exercises too often. Stick to this list for 12 weeks to master the "skill" of lifting before varying the movements.
